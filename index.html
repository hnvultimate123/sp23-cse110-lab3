Brainstorming

8-Ball

  1. Would be like a magic 8-ball, that gives set responses that are randomized
  2. Fairly simple to implement, can also be modified to have more responses easily
  3. Users would be primarily younger people who like to mess around, a "for fun" app
  4. Can include a graphic of an 8-ball, along with animations to improve the user experience visually

Example of interface shown above.

Fortune-Telling Seer

  1. Visually would appear as a seer or a person who can "see" the future through a crystal ball
  2. More difficult to implement in terms of UI elements, but also much greater potential for growth
  3. Can use some kind of AI to tailor a response based on prior user experiences with the app
  4. Similar audience as the 8-ball application

One primary concern voiced:
Ensuring that the user is not asking anything that could induce harm.
Some precautions may need to be taken in regard to this.
